=========================================
Pattern: Incremental Frequency Expansion
=========================================

Recognition:

Need an answer for every growing substring.

Core Idea:

Fix the left boundary.

Grow the right boundary one character
at a time.

Maintain the frequency array
incrementally.

Every extension creates a NEW substring.

↓

Update one frequency.

↓

Compute answer immediately.

Algorithm:

for every left

    freq = new int[26]

    for every right

        freq[currentChar]++

        compute max frequency

        compute min frequency
        (ignore zeros)

        answer += max - min

Loop Invariant:

After processing right,

freq[] stores the exact frequencies
of substring s[left...right].

Recognition Clues:

"Every substring"

"Frequency"

"Growing substring"

↓

Think:

Incremental Frequency Summary

Complexity:

Time:
O(n²)

Space:
O(1)

Common Mistakes:

❌ Rebuilding frequency array for every substring

❌ Including zero frequencies in minimum

❌ Computing beauty only once per left

❌ Using HashMap instead of int[26]

Golden Rule:

Growing substring

↓

Update ONE frequency

↓

Compute answer immediately

↓

Never rebuild the summary.
